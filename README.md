# Agile Coaching & Training Materials

## Overview

This repository contains sample Agile training and coaching materials used to mentor, train, and support teams and organizations adopting Agile frameworks. These resources were developed and used during Agile transformation initiatives to help teams improve collaboration, delivery efficiency, and Agile maturity.

The materials demonstrate practical experience facilitating Agile ceremonies, coaching cross-functional teams, and implementing scaled Agile frameworks within enterprise environments.

## Frameworks Covered

* Scrum
* SAFe (Scaled Agile Framework)
* Kanban
* LeSS (Large Scale Scrum)

## Repository Contents

* **SAFe Scrum Master Digital Workbook** – Training workbook used to guide teams through SAFe principles, Agile leadership practices, and PI Planning exercises.
* **Agile Coaching Materials** – Sample resources used to mentor Scrum Masters, Product Owners, and Agile teams.
* **Agile Workshops & Exercises** – Training exercises designed to improve team collaboration and Agile adoption.
* **Ceremony Facilitation Guides** – Materials used to guide Sprint Planning, Daily Standups, Sprint Reviews, and Retrospectives.
* **PI Planning Guidance** – Example materials used to support Program Increment planning sessions and cross-team coordination.

## Agile Coaching Focus Areas

* Coaching teams on Agile values, principles, and best practices
* Facilitating Scrum ceremonies and scaled Agile events
* Supporting Agile transformation initiatives
* Mentoring Scrum Masters and Product Owners
* Improving team collaboration, transparency, and delivery predictability

## Purpose

These materials demonstrate practical experience supporting Agile delivery and coaching teams in enterprise environments. They provide insight into the tools, frameworks, and training approaches used to guide organizations through Agile adoption and continuous improvement.
